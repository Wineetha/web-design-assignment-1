Step by Step to Becoming a Video Editor

  1. Strengthen my editing skills through university courses and practice.
  2. Learn software like Adobe Premiere Pro and DaVinci Resolve.
  3. Create a portfolio with edited videos and class projects.
  4. Join tutorials and online classes to expand knowledge.
  5. Apply for internships to gain hands-on industry experience.
  6. Start freelancing or full-time editing career post-graduation.
